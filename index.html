Tax figures are a simplified estimate of U.S. federal capital gains only: short-term gains (held < 1 year) are taxed at your ordinary income bracket; long-term gains (held ≥ 1 year) use the 0% / 15% / 20% rate roughly mapped from your bracket. It does not include state taxes, the 3.8% Net Investment Income Tax, wash-sale rules, cost-basis adjustments, deductions, or your actual marginal situation.
